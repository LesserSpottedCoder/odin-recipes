Roast Chicken

Description

Roast chicken is a hearty and well loved dish which can be served year round. It's as versatile as it is tasty, with the option to adapt the dish to suit any flavour profile you wish - herbed, spiced, simple seasoning etc.

Roast chicken is a wonderful alternative to the ever festive but stubbornly dry Christmas roast turkey. Juicy, succulent, and warming, who would choose anything else as their favourite dish!

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 2tbsp desired seasoning e.g mixed herbs
  • Thyme sprig
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees
  2. Remove giblets if present
  3. Place breast up in small roasting pan
  4. In a small bowl combine ingredients
  5. Rub mixture over top of chicken until fully coated
  6. Put chicken in oven and roast for approx. 90 minutes, depending on the size of bird
    7. Once cooked, a knife inserted into the chicken will result in clear juices
  7. Let chicken rest on countertop covered in foil for around 15 minutes before carving
